== Why Do You Need Product Reviews for WooCommerce Plugin? ==

Having a review plugin in your arsenal can help you in many ways. The Product Reviews for WooCommerce plugin will enhance the reviews and rating section on the product pages, and give you SEO benefits such as better CTR, better online visibility, and rich snippets on the SERPs.

Product Reviews Plugin extends the core features of WooCommerce review and rating to enhance its features. Your customers can do more than just write a boring review, they can upload media, rate product attributes, and much more. In short, it makes the review and rating section more interactive.

== Installation ==
 
= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Product Reviews for WooCommerce, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type **"Product Reviews for WooCommerce"** and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Product Reviews for WooCommerce plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly, of course, you can install it by simply clicking **"Install Now"**.

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our Product Reviews for WooCommerce and uploading it to your webserver via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [**Instructions on how to do this here**](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How will the plugin show reviews on my WooCommerce product page? =
The product review plugin allows you to collect the reviews in two ways that are review form on the Product Page or through a Pop-up form.

= Does the product review plugin have a feature to show reviews only on approval? =
Yes. This can be done by enabling “Approval required for review” from the General Settings Tab of the plugin which will give the functionality to show reviews on the product page from customers only after they are approved.

= Is it possible to upload product images along with sharing the reviews by the customer? =
Yes. This is the basic feature that is there in our WooCommerce Product Reviews plugin which allows the customers to upload the product images along with the review they share.

= Does the plugin provide the feature to boost the SEO of the product reviews submitted by the customers? =
Yes, we have also provided an automated feature to boost the SEO of your product reviews submitted by the customer. Now with the help of product attribute tags, the plugin helps in making the reviews visible on Google search results. This can be done by Enabling the SEO Boost in the General Settings of the plugin.

= What are the different types of SEO boost available in the plugin settings? =
There are two types of SEO boost facilities available using our plugin. These are MPN and GTIN respectively. You can also create the custom MPN or GTIN by enabling the “Create custom MPN/GTIN” and then entering the prefix in the “Enter Custom Prefix” area that opens on enabling the setting.

= Is there any other feature in the plugin to enable the brand for the SEO boosting facility? =
If the SEO boost setting is enabled then this plugin also provides the facility to enter the brand name in each of the products for SEO use. This can be simply done by enabling the setting “Enable Brand” and then entering the Brand Name in the space provided.

=  Is there any way to categorize and collect ratings for specific product attributes on every product page? =
Yes. We have the option in the General Settings Tab of our Plugin named “Review features” where you can add the feature that you want to be reviewed and click on + and then save the settings.

= Does the product review plugin have the facility to provide voting on the reviews done for the products? =
Product Reviews for WooCommerce plugin has the feature to Enable voting for reviews done by other customers where the other customers can vote the helpful or not helpful reviews for them. This can be done by enabling the “Enable Voting” from the General Settings Tab of the plugin.
